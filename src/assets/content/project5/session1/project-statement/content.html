Project Statement

PJ Martin investment firm is sponsoring a $10,000 scholarship for one female rising freshmen majoring in business. To qualify for the scholarship, in addition to an application and essay, you have to turn in a stock tracker and trend analysis.

Assignment

Create an Excel workbook that tracks your stocks over a predetermined period of time (days, weeks or months, as determined by your teacher). Create a scatter plot that visually demonstrates the trend of your stock and be sure to include a best fit line.
