Investigating Stock Data

Your teacher will place you into small groups and within these groups you will:

  • Create a Spreadsheet (Google or Excel) entitled
    Wells Fargo and American Express stock performance.
  • Each team will create a data table and graph of the performance of the two stocks over the following variables
    • Day : 9:00am, 10am, 11am (opening til closing)
    • Week: Monday, Tuesday, Wed, (Entire week)
    • Month : January, (Pick one month week by week)
    • Year : 12 months
    • Five Years: Each Year
