Calculating Profit and Loss.

To determine the amount of profit or loss:

  1. Calculate the amount paid for the stock by multiplying the number of shares by the purchase price per share.
  2. Calculate the amount the stock sold for by multiplying the number of shares by the selling price per share.
  3. Subtract the total of #1 from the total of #2. This amount is your gain or loss.
