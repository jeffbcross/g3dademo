Re-Read Project Statement

You notice that suspensions at your school are often given to the same group of students. You want to bring this up as a school wide issue that should be addressed by your peer-mentoring group.

Assignment

Prepare a 3-minute speech to your fellow mentors convincing them that there is unequal school discipline in different groups. Do this by summarizing a comparison of the interquartile range and standard deviation of out-of-school suspension data for two groups. For example, find and compare interquartile range and standard deviation of the percent of out-of-school suspensions for students with disabilities vs. students without disabilities or Asian students vs. Hispanic, etc.
